My Projects

Manga Hero

Narrative Designer, Level Designer, Sound Designer

Manga Hero is an upcoming 3D action game created in Unreal Engine 5 by TenByTen Studio at Duke University. Step into the shoes of Hiro, a high school jock, as he fights to restore balance in a world where reality and manga have collided. Experience a unique manga-inspired artstyle as you battle pirates, ninjas, robots, and more with various weapons. Planned for a spring 2026 release.

Lacrosse Dimensions

Gameplay Programmer

An out-of-this-world sports game created in one week for the 2025 Triangle Game Jam. Launch the ball into the enemy's goal while switching back and forth between your own goalie for defense. Master chaotic zero-gravity controls and harness the power of wormholes to become the lacrosse champion. Available now on itch.io!
